Welcome to TokenWise

Monitor and reduce your AI token usage — 100% locally

1

Real-time token tracking

TokenWise adds a floating widget to your AI chat pages. It counts tokens as you type and shows your full conversation cost — so you always know what you're about to send.

ChatGPT Claude Gemini
2

Smart optimization suggestions

As you write prompts, TokenWise analyzes your text locally and suggests ways to cut filler, redundancy, and formatting waste — with one-click Apply buttons and estimated token savings.

  • Detects polite filler and verbose phrasing
  • Flags duplicate instructions
  • Estimates cost for file attachments and pasted URLs
3

Your data never leaves your browser

TokenWise makes zero network requests. No telemetry, no cloud sync, no data exfiltration. Everything is stored in chrome.storage.local on your device and can be cleared anytime from Analytics.

  • Open the extension popup for session stats and settings
  • View Analytics for 7-day charts and savings reports
  • Drag the widget to reposition it; dismiss with ×

All data stays local. Nothing is sent to external servers.
